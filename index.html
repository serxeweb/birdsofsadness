About Us

Birds Of Sadness

Welcome to our Guild! We are a diverse and dynamic community of gamers, united by our passion for gaming and our commitment to excellence. Our guild was founded by Sho and Chow with the mission to create a supportive and inclusive environment where members can thrive, share their experiences, and achieve their gaming goals together.


Our journey began in February 23 2019, and since then, we have grown into a family of dedicated players from all over the world. We pride ourselves on our teamwork, strategic prowess, and the strong bonds we've formed both in-game and out. Our members are not just teammates; they are friends who support each other through every challenge and celebrate every victory.


Whether you're a seasoned veteran or a newcomer looking to improve your skills, our guild offers a place for everyone. We host regular events, training sessions, and social gatherings to ensure that every member feels valued and has the opportunity to contribute to our collective success. Join us and become part of a guild where your gaming journey matters.
